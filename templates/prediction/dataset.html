Class Descriptions

No DR

No signs of diabetic retinopathy detected.

Mild

Mild nonproliferative diabetic retinopathy with microaneurysms only.

Moderate

Moderate nonproliferative diabetic retinopathy with more than just microaneurysms but less than severe NPDR.

Severe

Severe nonproliferative diabetic retinopathy with any of the following: more than 20 intraretinal hemorrhages in each of four quadrants, definite venous beading in 2+ quadrants, or prominent IRMA in 1+ quadrant, and no signs of proliferative retinopathy.

Proliferative DR

Proliferative diabetic retinopathy with one or both of the following: neovascularization and/or vitreous/preretinal hemorrhage.
